The Fortune Teller Miracle Fish!

My first encounter with a Fortune Teller Miracle Fish was as a young girl one Christmas Day at my grandparents' house. I still remember the feeling of wonder on discovering, inside a Christmas cracker, the magical red fish who promised to see inside my soul...

How it works

Simply place the Fortune Teller Fish onto the palm of your hand, and its movements will tell you everything you need to know.

History

The Fortune Teller Miracle Fish dates back to at least the 1930s, although there are references to similar moving objects being used by magicians as early as the 18th century.

Controversy

Over the years there has been some speculation that the name "Miracle Fish" is a misnomer. Doubters claim that the mysterious movements of the tiny red fish can be explained by science. They are wrong.
